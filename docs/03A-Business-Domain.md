# Sydora AI OS - Business Domain

Version: 1.0

---

# Business Overview

Sydora AI OS is a multi-tenant AI-powered Digital Marketing Operating System.

The platform manages organizations, users, clients, projects, AI agents, marketing campaigns, reports, knowledge, workflows, and integrations.

---

# Core Business Objects

## Organization

Represents an agency or company using Sydora AI OS.

Examples

- Sydora Digital
- ABC Marketing
- XYZ Hotels

---

## Users

People who access the platform.

Roles

- Super Admin
- Agency Owner
- Manager
- SEO Executive
- Ads Executive
- Content Writer
- Client

---

## Clients

Businesses served by an organization.

Examples

- Hotel ABC
- XYZ Builders
- Smile Dental Clinic

---

## Projects

Every client can have multiple projects.

Examples

- SEO
- Google Ads
- Website Development
- Reputation Management

---

## AI Agents

Digital employees responsible for completing tasks.

Examples

- Master Agent
- SEO Agent
- Google Ads Agent
- GEO Agent
- AEO Agent
- Content Agent
- QA Agent

---

## Tasks

Every project is divided into tasks.

Examples

- Website Audit
- Keyword Research
- Create Landing Page
- Publish Blog

---

## Campaigns

Marketing campaigns.

Examples

- Google Ads
- Meta Ads
- SEO Campaign
- Email Campaign

---

## Knowledge

Shared organizational knowledge.

Includes

- SOPs
- Templates
- Checklists
- Brand Guidelines
- Case Studies
- Industry Playbooks

---

## Reports

Generated by AI Agents.

Examples

- SEO Report
- Google Ads Report
- Client Monthly Report

---

## Workflows

Automation sequences.

Examples

- New Client Onboarding
- SEO Audit Workflow
- Proposal Workflow
- Monthly Reporting

---

## Integrations

External services.

Examples

- Google Analytics
- Search Console
- Google Ads
- Meta
- Gmail
- Google Drive
- Zoho CRM
- WhatsApp
