# Sydora AI Platform – AI Workforce Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: Architecture
Author: Sydora Digital

---

# Purpose

This document defines the complete AI Workforce Architecture of Sydora AI Platform.

Instead of isolated AI prompts, Sydora AI Platform operates as an organization where specialized AI employees collaborate under a central orchestration engine.

Every AI Agent has:

- Department
- Role
- Mission
- Skills
- Memory
- Tools
- Knowledge Access
- Inputs
- Outputs
- KPIs
- SOPs
- Approval Rules
